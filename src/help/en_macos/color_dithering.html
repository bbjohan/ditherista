Color Dithering

Color dithering dithers images in color.

The quality of the color dithering depends highly on the chosen target palette:

  • How many colors the target palette has.
  • How closely the target palette's colors match the source image's colors in terms of brightness and color value.

Ditherers

Error Diffusion Dithering

Use the Jitter parameter to introduce randomization into the dithering, to make it less uniform. The serpentine toggle changes how the algorithm processes the pixels in the image. For most error diffusion ditherers turning serpentine on improves the dithering quality.

With color dithering, you will notice that some error diffusion dither approaches yield better results than others when it comes to matching the color distribution of the source image.

Ordered Dithering

Use the Jitter parameter to introduce randomization into the dithering, to make it less uniform.

When dithering in color, one of the big drawbacks of ordered dither approaches is that they may not be able to achieve a good overall color and brightness distribution as other dithering approaches, such as error diffusion.
